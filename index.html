Jujutsu Kaisen

Jujutsu Kaisen is Japanese manga and anime series made by Gege Akutami. The first manga chapter came out in March 5,2018 in Shueisha's Weekly Shonen Jump Magazine. The story follows with Yuji Itadori (The protaganist) joins a secret organization of Jujutsu Sorcercers to eliminate the powerful curse named Ryomen Sukuna that resides inside his body, as he became the host of such anomaly after consuming a cursed object of Sukuna.

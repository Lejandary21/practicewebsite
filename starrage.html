Star Rage


This technique allows the user to add virtual mass to herself, her shikigami Garuda. This add imaginary mass yet it is capable of causing mass destruction with its immense physical capabilities. The only notable user of this innate technique is Yuki Tsukumo


Garuda is a shikigami that is connected to Yuki's Technique, Star Rage. Garuda is a long, white cursed spirit with a unique appearance. It has a long, serpent-esque body with sections that are covered with bone-like columns. This same material comprises Garuda's head, which is complete with two eyes and a bird-like bill. It also possesses eight wings that levitate around its body and enable flight. Garuda can also be affected with mass to itself, making it more physically powerful.

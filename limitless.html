Limitless/ Six Eyes

Limitless is a technique is an inherited technique derived from the Gojo family. The user is immediately equipped with a Laplace Blue, a powerful extension of the technique that allows the user generate the power to increase cursed energy output to attract. Another extension of the tecnique is called Reversal Red. The technique manifests itself as small but a bright red orb on the tip of its user's finger. When released, the explosive output is massive. Creating a gigantic shockwave that blows everything away. The Six Eyes allows the user to see cursed energy in extreme detail. As such the user of the Six Eyes is able to see the flow of cursed energy, which in turn allows them to read another person's cursed techniques as well. Notable users are Gojo Satoru and an unknown ancient Gojo clan member.
